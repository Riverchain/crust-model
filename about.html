About WATUM development team

WATUM or Watershed Aqua Transportation Utility Model is a developing water management and modeling tool, intended to bring ease and advantages to hands of nations that are seeking water qaulity sequrity for their people all over the world.

WATUM is a free of charge, open-source tool for water quality management.
